**Headline:** Discover Turning Points — The Moments That Actually Define Breaking News

**The Problem:**
We're drowning in information but starving for insight. Every day, thousands of news articles compete for our attention—yet most are just variations of the same story. Traditional news aggregators (Feedly, Google News) pile articles on top of each other, leaving you to manually find connections, understand nuance, and decide what actually matters.

Result? Information overload. Echo chambers. Missing the real story.

**The Solution: SoWork News**

SoWork News is an **open-source AI-powered news aggregation platform** that solves this by understanding *what actually matters* in breaking news.

Instead of volume, we deliver insight.

**Three Core Innovations:**

1. **Turning Point Detection** — Our AI identifies the critical moments that define how news stories evolve. Not every article matters equally. Some shift markets. Others change policy. Some reveal surprising new angles. We find those moments automatically.

2. **Perspective Suggestions** — Breaking news comes with competing perspectives. Our AI analyzes articles from different sources and suggests discussion angles you might not see in your normal feed. Avoid echo chambers. Understand context.

3. **Story Clustering** — Same story, 50 different headlines. Our AI groups related articles so you see the complete picture without repetition.

**Key Features:**

### Common HN Questions (Prepared Responses)

**Q: How is this different from Feedly?**
```
Feedly is a feed reader—you add sources, get articles in a list. Very 2010.

SoWork is intelligent aggregation. Our AI understands the news, clusters similar stories, and identifies turning points. It's asking "what actually matters?" not "what did I subscribe to?"

The best analogy: Feedly is like email folders. SoWork is like having a smart news editor filtering for you.
```

**Q: How is this different from Perplexity?**
```
Perplexity is search. You ask a question, it finds sources and synthesizes an answer.

SoWork is continuous monitoring. We watch 50,000 sources in real-time and highlight moments that shift understanding or markets.

Different problem, different solution. Could definitely integrate with them.
```

**Q: How does the AI work?**
```
Three components:

1. **Vectorization** — Articles are converted to embeddings so we can measure semantic similarity
2. **Clustering** — Related articles grouped based on embeddings + metadata
3. **Turning Point Detection** — ML model trained on annotated news data to identify inflection moments

We use OpenAI embeddings + custom ranking algorithms. The turning point detection is the proprietary part—trained on ~5 years of historical news data.

Still being improved as we get real user feedback.
```
